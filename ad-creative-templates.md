# MONDIALE EN VILLE — Ad Creative Templates (Brian)

All copy in French. Tone: confident, sophisticated, footballistic. Never shouty.

## Brand colours for visuals
- Primary: `#1a7a45` (pitch green)
- Accent: `#c8102e` (competition red)
- Gold: `#b8960c` (premium / VIP)
- Navy: `#0c1a2e` (background)
- Bone: `#faf9f6` (light surface)

## Fonts
- Display: **Instrument Serif** (large headlines)
- Body: **Plus Jakarta Sans** (CTAs, captions)
- Numbers: **JetBrains Mono** (dates, prices, scores)

---

## Format 1 — Meta Feed (1080×1080, square)

**Visual**
- Background: dark navy with subtle pitch-green radial glow
- Centered: large white Instrument Serif text
- Top-left eyebrow badge: `MONDIAL FIFA 2026 · GENÈVE` (10px, letter-spacing 3px)
- Bottom-left: small pitch-green pill button "RÉSERVER →"

**Headline variants** (Instrument Serif, 120pt)
1. *Vivez la Coupe du Monde comme jamais.*
2. *Genève vibre au rythme du Mondial.*
3. *Trois lieux. Une seule passion.*
4. *Le football. La ville. L'expérience.*

**Body line** (Plus Jakarta Sans, 28pt, light)
- Cercle des Bains · Uptown Geneva · Optimhall
- Du 11 juin au 19 juillet 2026

---

## Format 2 — Meta / Instagram Story (1080×1920, vertical)

**Layout**
- Top third: noise-textured navy with stadium silhouette
- Middle third: huge Instrument Serif headline (clamp text into 3 lines max)
- Bottom third: countdown ("PLUS QUE 38 JOURS" in JetBrains Mono) + "RÉSERVEZ VOTRE PLACE" CTA pill

**Headline pool**
1. *La fanzone qui change tout.*
2. *Premium. Pas générique.*
3. *Ici, chaque match devient un moment.*

**Sticker overlays**
- Countdown sticker linked to first match
- Link sticker → `mondialeenville.ch/billetterie`

---

## Format 3 — TikTok / Reels (1080×1920, video, 9-15s)

**Hook (0-2s):** Close-up shot of beer being poured + crowd roar audio
**Beat 1 (2-5s):** Cut to wide shot of one of the three venues, text overlay: "GENÈVE · 2026"
**Beat 2 (5-9s):** Quick cuts between three venues with location name appearing
**Payoff (9-13s):** Static brand card with logo + "RÉSERVEZ MAINTENANT"
**End card (13-15s):** URL + QR code

**On-screen text style:** All caps, JetBrains Mono, white with subtle drop shadow

---

## Format 4 — Google Display / YouTube banner (1200×628)

**Layout (3-column logic)**
- Left: brand logo + "MONDIALE EN VILLE 2026"
- Center: Instrument Serif headline
- Right: pitch-green CTA button "BILLETTERIE"

**Headline:** *La Coupe du Monde se vit à Genève.*
